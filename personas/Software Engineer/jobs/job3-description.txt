<h3>Job Description</h3>
<p><em>Join Origin Energy as a Software Engineer and contribute to building and maintaining critical systems that power commercial customers and support Australia’s transition to renewable energy.</em></p>
<p>We are seeking a motivated Software Engineer to join our Melbourne-based team. You’ll work in an Agile environment, delivering high-quality solutions, developing and maintaining IT systems, and collaborating across teams to ensure seamless system integrations.</p>

<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
<li>Develop, enhance, and maintain IT systems, including CRM and billing platforms, ensuring continuity for commercial customers.</li>
<li>Deliver new features and solutions using Java (Spring, Hibernate, Maven), React, Python, MongoDB, and MS SQL.</li>
<li>Support day-to-day IT operations, incident management, and system maintenance.</li>
<li>Collaborate with teams to integrate systems and deliver positive customer outcomes.</li>
<li>Participate in after-hours support roster as Incident Manager, monitoring major incidents.</li>
</ul>

<h4>To Be Successful in this Role</h4>
<ul>
<li>Experience in software development within Agile/sprint-based teams.</li>
<li>Strong skills in Java (Spring, Hibernate, Maven) and React front-end development.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Python and databases (MS SQL, MongoDB).</li>
<li>Experience using Jira, Git, Docker, and CI/CD tools.</li>
<li>Familiarity with ITIL or similar frameworks and incident management.</li>
<li>Proactive problem-solving and strong communication skills.</li>
<li>Willingness to participate in occasional after-hours support.</li>
</ul>
