<h3>Company Description</h3>
<p>Mainpac, established in 1984, is an Australian software vendor providing enterprise asset management (EAM) solutions across Australia and 23 international markets. We serve asset-intensive industries including manufacturing, mining, ports, utilities, government, and facilities management. As a 100% subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc. via Vela APX, we benefit from strong corporate support and international expertise.</p>
<p>Mainpac offers on-premise and on-demand EAM solutions complemented by consulting, implementation, training, and support services.</p>
