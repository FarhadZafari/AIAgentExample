<h3>About the Business</h3>
<p><strong>WA Country Builders</strong> is Western Australia's most awarded regional builder and the largest country builder in the State. From Kalbarri in the Mid-West to Albany in the Great Southern and out past Northam in the Greater Perth region, we proudly bring choice to the regions by offering home designs from four JWH Group brands: Plunkett Homes, The Rural Building Co, Residential Attitudes, and WA Country Builders.</p>
