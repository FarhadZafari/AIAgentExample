<h3>Company Description</h3>
<p>The City of Port Phillip is an inner-city council with around 1000 employees delivering more than 100 programs and services to residents, businesses, and visitors. These range from childcare and immunisations, to libraries, management of parks and open spaces, rubbish collection, road maintenance, and more. The City of Port Phillip has zero tolerance for child abuse and is a committed Child Safe organisation.</p>

<h3>Job Description</h3>
<p><em>Transform data into actionable insights. Support strategic decision-making. Drive impact.</em></p>
<p>We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. In this role, you’ll help shape data-informed decision-making across the organisation by developing dashboards, ensuring data integrity, and delivering actionable insights to stakeholders. You’ll work with advanced tools like Power BI, R, and Python while collaborating across departments to support strategic projects and initiatives.</p>

<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Collaborate with stakeholders to gather and define reporting and data requirements.</li>
    <li>Design and maintain dashboards and reports using Power BI and ERP tools.</li>
    <li>Analyse data using Python and R for deeper insights and automation.</li>
    <li>Cleanse and manage data to ensure accuracy, consistency, and security.</li>
    <li>Support testing activities and maintain documentation for ongoing improvements.</li>
</ul>

<h4>To Be Successful in this Role</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Proven experience developing end-to-end BI solutions and reports.</li>
    <li>Proficient in Power BI, DAX, R, and Python for data analysis and modelling.</li>
    <li>Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to manage large, complex datasets.</li>
    <li>Knowledge of data governance and ITIL tools like ServiceNOW.</li>
    <li>Experience with TechnologyOne ERP systems and Azure cloud platforms is favourable.</li>
</ul>
