<h3>Job Description</h3>
<p><em>Transform healthcare with data. Drive strategy. Improve lives.</em></p>
<p>Epworth is seeking a passionate and experienced Data Scientist to join our Strategy & Performance team. In this pivotal role, you’ll harness the power of data to shape strategic decisions, enhance patient outcomes, and optimise hospital operations.</p>

<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Develop predictive models and advanced analytics to support strategic initiatives.</li>
    <li>Analyse clinical, operational, and financial data to uncover trends and opportunities.</li>
    <li>Build automation and optimisation tools to improve hospital performance.</li>
    <li>Design interactive dashboards and visualisations for executive decision-making.</li>
    <li>Translate complex data into actionable insights for non-technical stakeholders.</li>
    <li>Collaborate across departments to ensure data integrity and alignment with goals.</li>
    <li>Lead research into emerging healthcare analytics trends and technologies.</li>
</ul>

<h4>To Be Successful in this Role</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Advanced analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
    <li>Emotional maturity, integrity, and sound judgment.</li>
    <li>Ability to deal with ambiguity and change.</li>
    <li>Commitment to excellence in patient safety and continuous improvement.</li>
    <li>Strong work ethic, adaptability, and prioritisation skills.</li>
    <li>Confidence in stakeholder engagement and collaboration.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Qualifications & Experience</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Tertiary qualification in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.</li>
    <li>4+ years in data science with healthcare analytics experience.</li>
    <li>Expertise in predictive modelling, statistics, and machine learning.</li>
    <li>Proficiency in Python, R, SQL, Power BI.</li>
    <li>Understanding of hospital performance metrics and financial optimisation.</li>
    <li>Excellent communication and strategic thinking skills.</li>
</ul>
