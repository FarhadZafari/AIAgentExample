<h3>About Kip Happy Stays</h3>
<p>Kip Happy Stays is Australia’s leading provider of pet care services, setting the benchmark for excellence and ensuring every pet receives the ultimate care and enrichment during their stay. As a national operator, our head office support team is remote-first, and we welcome applications from anywhere within Australia.</p>

<h3>The Role</h3>
<p>The People & Culture Advisor plays a vital role in supporting and advising employees and managers across our business. You’ll ensure timely, accurate, and effective people management while contributing to projects and continuous improvement initiatives.</p>

<h4>Duties & Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Manage and respond to HR enquiries through inboxes and comms channels (primarily Slack).</li>
    <li>Support with HR matters including performance management, health & safety, recruitment, probation, workers compensation, and termination.</li>
    <li>Escalate complex people matters to the Head of People & Culture.</li>
    <li>Support implementation and communication of HR policies and procedures.</li>
    <li>Contribute to HR projects and continuous improvement initiatives.</li>
    <li>Maintain the HRIS and assist with contracts, policies, and employee records.</li>
    <li>Coordinate HR activities and calendar for smooth operations.</li>
    <li>Assist with end-to-end recruitment and facilitate onboarding for new hires.</li>
    <li>Support payroll team with accurate pay runs.</li>
    <li>Prepare HR reports, correspondence, and information as required.</li>
    <li>Model Kip’s values of Fun, Compassion, Honesty, Expertise, and Achievement.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Experience & Skills</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Tertiary qualification in HR, business, employment law, learning & development, or relevant experience.</li>
    <li>Knowledge of HR principles, policies, and practices.</li>
    <li>Strong stakeholder engagement and relationship-building skills.</li>
    <li>Ability to work autonomously and collaboratively across diverse teams.</li>
    <li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
    <li>Proficiency with Google Suite programs.</li>
    <li>Professionalism, strong work ethic, and willingness to learn.</li>
    <li>Passion for pet care and animal welfare.</li>
</ul>

<h4>How to Apply</h4>
<p>Include a cover letter outlining why you’re the best fit for the role — even if you don’t tick every box, we’d love to hear how your skills can transfer. Shortlisted candidates will complete a brief one-way video interview as the first stage. Successful candidates will undergo a National Criminal History Check (funded by Kip).</p>

<p>For queries, contact us at <a href="mailto:jobs@kip.com.au">jobs@kip.com.au</a>. We look forward to your application!</p>
