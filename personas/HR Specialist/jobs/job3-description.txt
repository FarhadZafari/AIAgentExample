<h3>About the Opportunity</h3>
<p>Are you passionate about creating a thriving workplace culture and driving meaningful change? RACV is seeking a dynamic People & Culture Consultant to be a trusted partner across our business, delivering high-impact HR support and continuous improvement.</p>
<p>As the primary contact for people matters, you’ll play a pivotal role in delivering professional, responsive, and cohesive People & Culture services. You will work closely with stakeholders to streamline processes, enhance system functionality, and champion operational excellence.</p>

<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Identify and implement continuous improvement of P&C processes, content, and systems to optimise usability, accuracy, and efficiency.</li>
    <li>Contribute to People & Culture related projects.</li>
    <li>Provide professional and efficient support to the business, ensuring accurate and timely execution of P&C administrative tasks, processes, and queries.</li>
    <li>Develop key stakeholder relationships, working in partnership to ensure business needs are met.</li>
</ul>

<h4>What You’ll Need to Be Successful</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Relevant qualification in People & Culture or related discipline.</li>
    <li>At least 2 years’ experience working within a P&C function.</li>
    <li>Demonstrated HR process improvement experience, P&C system knowledge, and ability to provide P&C advice.</li>
    <li>Intermediate Microsoft Office suite skills.</li>
    <li>Excellent administration, time management, and organisational skills.</li>
    <li>Strong understanding of positive customer service principles and results-focused mindset.</li>
    <li>High attention to detail and strong communication skills.</li>
    <li>Customer-focused and collaborative approach.</li>
</ul>
