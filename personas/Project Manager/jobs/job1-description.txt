<h3>Job Description</h3>
<p><em>Step into a role where growth meets opportunity, with a clear path to leadership and the chance to drive your career forward!</em></p>
<p>Manuplex is seeking an experienced Underground Mining Supervisor / Ventilation Coordinator to lead teams, ensure compliance, and drive operational excellence across underground coal projects.</p>

<h4>Key Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
<li>Attend daily planning and handover meetings with Project Managers, Supervisors, and other teams.</li>
<li>Communicate task requirements, equipment needs, and operational documents ahead of each shift.</li>
<li>Conduct short-term planning (daily/weekly) and scope future projects to align resources and execution methods.</li>
<li>Perform underground inspections for planning, auditing, and maintaining compliance with mine site standards.</li>
<li>Liaise with ERZ Controllers to ensure ventilation and construction tasks are completed on time and within compliance.</li>
<li>Lead, mentor, support, and train crew members, particularly new or inexperienced operators.</li>
<li>Monitor work area environments and ensure adherence to environmental and safety standards.</li>
<li>Promote a strong safety culture through toolbox talks, hazard identification, risk assessments, and incident reporting.</li>
<li>Oversee and participate in the installation and maintenance of ventilation devices, strata support, and roof bolting.</li>
<li>Drive team performance by providing timely feedback, fostering accountability, and encouraging continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
